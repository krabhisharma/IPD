Guess a Number From 1 to 20
INSTRUCTION
The game is to guess a random number generated by computer in range 1 ‐ 20 in minimum number of Guesses.
  • The computer is going to randomly select an integer from 1 to 20.
  • You'll keep guessing numbers until you find the computer's number, and the computer will tell you each time if your guess was too high or too low
  • Total score will be no of times you have tried
